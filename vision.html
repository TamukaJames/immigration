Introduction


As the registrar General's Office in the Republic of Zimbabwe as stated by sect 123(a) of the consitution of the republic of Zimbabwe Operating under the ministry of home affairs, we aim to provide the best services for the general Zimbabwean Public when it comes to immigration.

Vision

To be the centre of excellence in the production of travel and vital civil registration documents

Mission

To deliver efficient service in the processing of births and deaths, registration of brands, marriages, citizenship, national identity documents, designing, manufacturing, processing and issuance of travel documents.

Core values

In carrying out its mandate the department is guided by the following core values:

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Professionalism
  • Impartiality
  • Timeless

RG's Message
